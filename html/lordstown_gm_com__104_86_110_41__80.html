Welcome to Lordstown


General Motors Lordstown Complex is the proud maker of the Chevrolet CRUZE. Lordstown is among the highest volume single line vehicle assembly facilities in the world with total production at over 16 million vehicles since production started in 1966. On September 8, 2010, the complex officially launched and started shipping the Chevrolet Cruze which is today among the world’s best-selling vehicles. Lordstown has built over 1.3 million Cruzes since launch. On June 24, 2015, the Next Generation Cruze was revealed to the world in Detroit. Lordstown begins shipping the All New 2016 Cruze in Spring 2016.

Most Lordstown team members are represented by United Autoworker Locals 1112 and 1714.

Each year, Lordstown employees contribute over $1 million annually to various community causes including local United Way chapters, March of Dimes of Northern Ohio, Toys for Kids and Care & Share to help families in need. Click here to view video.

Lordstown Complex is located off the Ohio Turnpike (Interstate 80), twenty miles west of the Ohio-Pennsylvania border.
