Vehicle Inspection and Reconditioning

This is not your regular, everyday used vehicle. Certified Pre-Owned status is a name that is earned, and it is earned only by meeting our strict, factory-set standards. If it doesn't pass, your Certified Chevy, Buick, or GMC dealer reconditions it, so when you buy Certified you can expect higher standards.

Important Recall Information: Before a Certified Pre-Owned vehicle is listed or sold, GM requires dealers to complete all safety recalls. However, because even the best processes can break down, we encourage you to check the recall status of any vehicle at recalls.gm.com.
