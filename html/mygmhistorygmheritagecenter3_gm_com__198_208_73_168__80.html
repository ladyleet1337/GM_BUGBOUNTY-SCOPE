GM's Online Living History

When we set out to celebrate GM's first 100 years in 2008, we wanted to capture GM's history as told by the people that have worked for GM, as well as those whose lives have been touched by our products. To do that, we created the GMnext wiki. Thanks to our contributors, we have a very unique and robust account of GM's story that could not have been written in any one book by any one author.

The GMnext wiki was frozen in May of 2010 and is currently available as the Generations of GM History as part of our GM Heritage Center website.

We hope you enjoy this rich account of our history that so many helped create.
